The Advantages of Private Lessons

Why private lessons? How is it different from a classroom lesson?

Which students are better suited to private lessons?

Private Lesson or Class Lesson?

Private Lesson is One tutor teaching to One student

Is a private lesson better than a classroom lesson for learning a foreign language?

In a private lesson, the tutor’s focus is on only one student.

However, in a classroom lesson, one tutor is teaching many students. It is more difficult to maintain control and focus because some students learn faster than others.

Another benefit of private lessons is that the tutor comes to the student’s place. Also, when the student needs to reschedule a lesson, they only need to adjust the lesson time with the tutor.

Whereas, for a classroom lesson, the student must travel to the school to attend the lesson which is at a fixed time every week. If the students is not able to attend the class, they will miss it.

Which students are better suited to private lessons?

  • Not suited to Class Lessons

    The students might wonder “Am I suited to Private Lessons or Class Lessons?” before they decide to study a language. Some students are more suited to Class Lessons and some students are more suited to Private Lessons. If the student feels he/she is a fast-learner, or they’d like to practice more oral language skills, they should probably attend individual lessons.

  • Want to improve oral language skills after Class Lesson

    Attending Class Lessons with lots of classmates seems happier. However individual’s oral language practice time is limited in a classroom. In a Private Lesson, the student will have more opportunity to speak with his (her) private tutor and, whenever the student makes a mistake, he (she) will get corrected by the tutor immediately.

  • A private tutor is aware of the student’s weaknesses and is able to tailor his/her teaching to the individual.

    Some students are too shy to ask questions in class, especially in front of foreign tutors and some students might worry they will be laughed at by their classmates when they ask silly questions in class. However, these students feel free to ask ques tions of a private tutor.

What is the Disadvantage of Private Lessons?

A less competitive environment

What is the disadvantage of Private Lessons？The student might think the biggest disadvantage of Private Lessons is the higher tuition fee as compared to the price of a Class Lesson. But, it’s worth paying more if the student makes more progress in an individual learning environment.

The other disadvantage of Private Lessons is the student has no partner to learn with, in another words there is no competition environment, which will not help the student have more motivation on their learning. Especially for the exam preparation lesson.
