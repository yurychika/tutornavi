About Service

For Tutor

  • Point
    1

    Publishing of Tutors’ Profiles

    Tutors’ Profiles will be published once they are approved. The tutor’s basic information, lesson details, schedule, etc., are required. Tutors may edit their profile and change the privacy setting at their mypage.

  • Point
    2

    Create Blog at Tutor Mypage

    All registered tutors may create their own blog to promote themselves.Write any thoughts about lessons, frequent language mistakes by the students, Grammar Talk, etc. Once the blog is approved, it will be published on TutorNavi.Also, the tutors will be able to set the privacy level and manage the blog comments on the blog.

  • Point
    3

    Search for Students

    Registered tutors will be able to apply for the jobs which were posted by Platilum Students for Free of Charge by clicking button 「Apply」,then will get the respond message from the students.By upgrading tutors membership to platinum tutor account,tutors will be able to apply for all the jobs listed.For more info please check Membership Features

  • Point
    4

    Apply for Part time Jobs

    Recruiting Posts from companies and schools will be published on the Job Information section of Tutor’s mypage. Recruiting job posts include translation, interpreter, factory visits, client and company visits. Tutors will be able to apply for the job which were posted by the platilum plan for Free of Charge. Company clients recruit a tutor by reading through tutor profiles and tutor blogs to select a candidate.

  • Point
    5

    Reply to Questions at 「Community」

    Registered tutors will be able to reply to questions from the students. This is one way to earn a solid reputation and garner response from the students.
