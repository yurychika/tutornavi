How to Make a Comment

What is the TutorNavi Commenting Section?

The TutorNavi Commenting Section is a place to give feedback about tutors’ Blogs.
Comments you leave about the tutors’ blogs are public to all readers.
Please read the commenting rules before posting!

Commenting Rules

Only registered members can leave a comment. If you are a member of TutorNavi, please login first, then you can leave a comment.
Your meaningful comments will encourage the tutors to continute their blogs and share with the readers.

Even if you disagree with someone’s point of view, please try to be tactful about it.
Whenever you decide to leave a comment on a blog post, please ask yourself if your comments will be useful to all the readers.
Please read the following guidelines

  • Please leave only pertinent comments
  • Do not leave the irrelevant comments about Tutor Blog
  • Do not promote yourself or your blog, services, etc.
  • Do not harass the blog owner or other members who have made comments
  • Failure to comply with these rules may result in a ban from commenting or the entire site, as well as removal of posts and points!
