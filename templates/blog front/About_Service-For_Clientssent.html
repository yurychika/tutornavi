  • Want an interpreter for an overnight factory visit
  • Want a bilingual local guide for sightseeing
  • Want a native language tutor for company training
  • Want an interpreter for a seminar or business meeting

About Service

  • Job posts will be published immediately（takes up to 2 working days.)
  • Since all bilingual native speakers are registered, you will definitely find the right person for your company
  • Easy to find the right interpreter for your business documents and exhibition assistance
  • You will be able to make a good choice of an interpreter by interviewing several candidates

Plan and Pricing

  • ① Sliver Plan (Posting FREE)
    Can not initiate contact with other members.
    Can only reply to platinum members who have applied for the job you have posted.
  • ② Platinum Plan
    Your job posting information will be sent to all the registered tutors’ registered email address.
    Can reply to all the members who have applied for the job you have posted.
    We confirm the amount, your job post will be up to 2 working days published to the site.

For platinum plan, once we confirm the amount, the job post will be published to the site.
