Japanese Language Proficiency Test : JLPT

General

The Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) has been offered by the Japan Foundation and Japan Educational Exchanges and Services (formerly Association of International Education, Japan) since 1984 as a reliable means of evaluating and certifying the Japanese proficiency of non-native speakers.

Test Results

Level Summary Test Sections and Test Time Comparison with Old Tests
N1 Able to understand Japanese used in a variety of circumstances. Language Knowledge Vocabulary・Grammar/Reading 110 min Listening 60 min Approximately the same level as the old Level 1 Test, but designed to measure slightly more advanced abilities.
N2 Able to understand Japanese used in everyday situations, and in a variety of circumstances to a certain degree. Language Knowledge Vocabulary・Grammar/Reading 105 min Listening 50 min Approximately the same level as the old Level 2 Test.
N3 Able to understand Japanese used in everyday situations to a certain degree. Language Knowledge Vocabulary 30 min Grammar・Reading 70 min Listening 40 min Positioned at a Level bridging the old Level 2 and Level 3 Tests.
N4 Able to understand basic Japanese. Language Knowledge Vocabulary 30 min Grammar・Reading 60 min Listening 35 min Approximately the same level as the old Level 3 Test.
N5 Able to understand some basic Japanese. Language Knowledge Vocabulary 25 min Grammar・Reading 50 min Listening 30 min Approximately the same as the old Level 4 Test.
