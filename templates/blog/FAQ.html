About TutorNavi

  • Q1: What is the TutorNavi Platform?
    A1: TutorNavi is a website platform which connects language tutors with students and provides clients with interpreters and translators. Tutors may create a profile, write blogs and interact with students in the community. Students can reply to Platinum Members who have contacted you first for free of charge, or upgrade to Platinum membership to contact unlimited members. Once they have chosen a tutor, private lessons can be held at a café, shop or via the Internet.
