English Language Test : TOEIC

General

Test of English for International Communications (TOEIC) is an English language test designed specifically to measure the everyday English skills of people working in an international environment. The US-based Educational Testing Service (ETS) developed the TOEIC test to measure achievement in using English in a business setting. The TOEIC Listening & Reading Test is a two-hour multiple-choice test consisting of 200 questions evenly divided into listening comprehension and reading comprehension. The TOEIC Speaking & Writing Test was introduced in 2006. The Speaking test assesses pronunciation, vocabulary, grammar, and fluency, while the Writing test examines vocabulary, grammar, and overall coherence and organization. The tests are designed to reflect actual English usage in the workplace, though they do not require any knowledge of specialized business terms.

Exam Results

TOEIC Proficiency Scale will be divided into A, B, C, D, E according to what scores you have achieved in the exams. The candidate TOEIC score and communication level can be checked in the following guideline.

Level TOEIC Score Assessment Guidelines
A Over 860 Score Fluent Communication as a Non-native speaker Able to communicate effectively in any situation.
B 730-855 Score Communicate in any situation without difficulties Able to satisfy most work requirements with language that is often, but not always, acceptable and effective.
C 470-725 Score Be able to communicate on daily life Able to satisfy most social demands and limited work requirements.
D 220-465 Score With limited conversation level Speaker has functional, but limited proficiency. Able to maintain very simple face-to-face conversations on familiar topics.
E -215 Score Very limited conversation level Able to communicate using simple words, have problems speaking English

Application procedure for TOEIC in HK

HK TOEIC Official Site

English Version (http://www.toeic.com.hk/English/index.html#)

  • (1) Click「English」on the right top、click「Register Now」

  • (2) 「TOEIC Registration Details」will show below
    Click「Register Online Now」

  • (3) Check「I Accept」at the bottom of the Terms of Use, click「Next」the next page will show below.

    Select “Listening and Reading” or “Speaking and Writing”.

  • (4) Select what exams you are going to take. Click「Next」the applicable exam date and location will show below.

  • (5) Next page with「Register or Login」
    Click「Register」

    Please fill in the following items

    • Name
    • ID Number(Passport is OK)
    • Date of Birth, Gender, Nationality
    • Email Address
    • Password
    • Telephone
    • Resident
    • School Name/Study

  • (6) After filling out your personal information, the exam fees may be paid online by credit card.

  • (7) Application Complete
    After your online payment, you will receive an email with the detailed exam location and time.
