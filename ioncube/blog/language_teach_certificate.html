English Tutoring Certificate

What is the difference between English teaching certificates?

Note：

The following four teaching certificates refer to teaching English to non-English speakers.

  • ※TESL(Teaching English as a Second Language)
    TESL refers to teaching English to students whose first language is not English, usually offered in a region where English is the dominant language(eg：Hong Kong, Singapore, etc)

  • ※TEFL(Teaching English as a Foreign Language)
    TEFL refers to teaching English to students whose first language is not English, usually in a region where English is not the dominant language(eg：Japan, China, etc.)

  • ※TEFL /TESL/TESOL (Teaching English as a Foreign / Second / to Speakers of Other Language) are internationally recognized qualifications that enable people/English teachers to Teach English as a Foreign or Second Language.

  • ※CELTA(Certificate in English Language Teaching to Adults) is an initial credential for teachers of English as a foreign language.
