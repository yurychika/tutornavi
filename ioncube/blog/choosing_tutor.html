  • Advice for Tutors

  • Check “Where is the tutor willing to travel to teach?”

    Hong Kong consists of Hong Kong Island, the Kowloon Peninsula, the New Territories, and over 200 offshore islands, of which the largest is Lantau Island. It is easy and convenient to travel around HK.. However, not all the tutors registered with TutoNavi are willing to travel all around HK..Some tutors prefer to teach closer to their home or workplace.Check tutor’s personal profile carefully to find a suitable tutor.

  • Check “Is the tutor qualified?”

    TutorNavi provides a service to help tutors and students connect and to help students find the perfect language tutor.However, not all the native foreign language tutors are qualified to be tutors. Here, we list the following points to help the student find a suitable tutor.

    Is the tutor who’s graduated from a top university always a qualified tutor? The answer is “NO”.

    A qualified tutor should be good at communication, have a great personality and posses a strong passion for teaching.

  • Check “What is the tutor’s teaching method?”

    Before the lesson, the student is able to ask the tutor questions using our internal messaging system. Remember to ask the tutor what their method of teaching is. Because every student is unique, and their reasons for learning a language may be different, a great teaching method may not fit every student. It’s important to make sure the tutor’s teaching style suits the student.

  • Check “Does the tutor has teaching experience at a language school?”

    The tutors registered with TutorNavi have varying teaching experiences and teaching styles. We cannot expect perfect results from every tutor. all perfect teaching resuls from all the registered tutors.The tutor who has had experience teaching at language schools is more likely to have a well-trained teaching style.

  • Check “Tutor’s age,interests and character?”

    By choosing a tutor who is in the same age range, with similar interests, the student will have more topics to discuss with the tutor in order to practice speaking.. Also, the student will feel free to ask the tutor more questions.

  • Check “Tutor’s blogs and comments, etc.”

    The student will get to know the tutor better by reading the blogs which are written by the tutor and by reading other students’ comments about the tutor.

  • Check “When is the tutor available?”

    Not all the tutors are available 24 hours, check tutor’s schedule for teaching on tutor’s profile.
